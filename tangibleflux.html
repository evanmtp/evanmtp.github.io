TANGIBLEFLUX φ PLENUMORPHIC ∴ CHAOSMOSIS

Navid Navab
Project link
Video by Navid Navab

Triptych of kinetic audio-visual-haptic sculptures investigating emergence of order out of chaos via the laws of complex harmonic motion.

Role

I contributed to the software that controls the sculptures' embedded mechanical, sound and lighting systems, allowing compositional control over hundreds of parameters. My work also facilitated system configuration, network communication and long-term reliability during exhibitions.
